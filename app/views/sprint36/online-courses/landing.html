Learning and training for furlough

If your employer puts you on temporary leave (furlough) because of coronavirus, you could be paid 80% of your wages while you’re off work. HM Revenue and Customs’ guidance can help you understand if you’re eligible.

The Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) are offering free online learning courses with their providers to give furlough workers in England the opportunity to improve existing skills or explore new ones.

Whether you are looking to progress your career or looking for a good use of your time during furlough, there are many benefits to learning online. An online course can help you develop a skill or interest you have.

The online courses range in topic and from levels 1 to 5 qualifications, see our qualification guidance if you’re unsure what course is best suited to you.
